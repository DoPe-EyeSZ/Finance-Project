Entries Explained

What Are Entries?

Entries represent income periodsâ€”like your paycheck or monthly budget. Each entry shows:

  • Total income added
  • How that income was automatically split across your expense categories
  • Money you spent from each category
  • Leftover money not assigned to any expense
  • Your total balance for that specific entry
  • How much you saved
